Soyabean

Incredient required

  • soya chunks
  • garlic powder
  • tomatos
  • salt
  • chilli
  • onion

Procedure

  1. Cut the onion and tomatos in small pieces and gring together to get a paste
  2. Prepare a Frying pan put 2 table spoon of oil and make it hot
  3. Fry the grinded paste put salt and chilli required
  4. Now put the soya chunks and cook for about 10 minutes
  5. Your dish is ready to serve
