MoMo

MoMo also known as dumplings, is famous nepalifood

Incredient required

  • Wheat flour
  • garlic powder
  • kima
  • salt
  • pepper
  • vegetables

Procedure

  1. Prepare a dough with the mixing of Wheat flour and garlic poweder
  2. mix vegetables and kima and add salt requierd
  3. Now roll the dough to get flat sheet and then cut the sheet with round sharp tool
  4. Fill the round sheets with the kima
  5. Steam the result to get tasty dumplins
